About SolaraForge

☀ Our Name

SolaraForge combines "Solar" (our energy source) with "Forge" (creation through fire and intensity). We're forging a new energy future for South Africa.

⚡ The Problem

South Africa faces daily load shedding, rising electricity prices, and environmental damage from coal-based power generation.

🌱 Our Solution

Reliable, affordable, and smart solar power systems that keep the lights on when the grid goes down.

👥 Who We Serve

From families in suburban homes to big industrial companies — anyone tired of sitting in the dark.

How We Source: From China to South Africa
