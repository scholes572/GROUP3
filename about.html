Our Story

Born from crisis, built for hope. How 7 students turned South Africa's biggest problem into its brightest opportunity.

☀️ The Name

SolaraForge combines "Solar" (our energy source) with "Forge" (creation through fire and intensity). We're forging a new energy future for South Africa.

⚡ The Problem

South Africa faces a perfect storm: daily load shedding (scheduled blackouts), rising electricity prices, and environmental damage from coal-based power generation. Families sit in darkness. Businesses lose revenue. The grid is failing.

🌱 The Solution

We harness the Northern Cape's 2,500+ annual sun hours to deliver reliable, affordable, smart solar power. Our systems don't just reduce bills-they eliminate load shedding entirely.

Load Shedding

Daily power cuts disrupting life and business across South Africa.

Rising Costs

Electricity prices increasing faster than inflation, hurting families.

Climate Crisis

Coal power contributing to global warming and air pollution.

Our Journey

Selected Northern Cape for manufacturing (sun hours) and Johannesburg for HQ (customer access).

Supply Chain

Launch

First installations completed. Families and businesses experiencing energy independence.
